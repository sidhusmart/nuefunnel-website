### A subsection

Use `###` for subsections under `##`. The blog detail page styles these consistently.

> Use blockquotes for emphasised statements or pull quotes — they get a distinct visual treatment in the prose styling.

## Including an image

Images dropped into `public/images/uploads/` are automatically optimised at build time (WebP variants at 400 / 800 / 1200 widths). Reference them with the absolute path:

![Descriptive alt text for accessibility and SEO](/images/uploads/example-image.png)

Note: images won't render in github.dev's Markdown preview pane (the preview can't resolve `/images/...` paths). They render correctly in the Cloudflare branch preview and in production.
