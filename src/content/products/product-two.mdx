WACAO started as a hack. We built it at a TechCrunch hackathon in 2017, duct-taping together libraries to bend WhatsApp to our will.

We've rebuilt it for the AI era. The new WACAO runs a private AI model on your device - nothing leaves your computer. So it can auto-translate messages, summarize the chatter in busy groups, and act as your assistant inside WhatsApp.

Guest houses and BnBs use it to talk to guests across languages. Small businesses use it to automate their customer chats. And anyone buried in noisy groups can use it just to catch up in a few seconds.
