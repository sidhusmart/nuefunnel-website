We built Podsumo to keep ourselves updated on all things AI and then realized it works for any podcast with an RSS feed. There is a lot of valuable information in podcast conversations but also a lot of noise.

Podsumo is a personalized podcast assistant that keeps track of podcasts and provides you with a summary in minutes, the moment an episode drops. It even reads across languages - point it at a Mandarin show and your summary lands in English. Not sure it's worth a listen? Ask it questions first and decide in seconds.

It lives in Telegram, with an MCP server and a private RSS feed so it slots into whatever you already use.
