### _**How a Leading Wholesaler Transformed Customer Engagement with AI-Powered WhatsApp Commerce**_

A leading B2B wholesaler operating across 100+ physical locations in Germany partnered with us at **NueFunnel** to modernize its customer engagement strategy. Serving millions of customers - from restaurants to local retailers - the client was looking to unify its fragmented outreach ecosystem and build a **scalable, AI-powered messaging channel** centered on WhatsApp.

Their goal:  
**“Let customers discover weekly offers, view catalogs, and receive timely updates - all inside WhatsApp.”**

But scaling this vision across diverse customer segments required more than just messaging infrastructure. It demanded **automation intelligence**.

#### **The Opportunity**

Customers were previously reached via a patchwork of flyers, emails, app promos, and sales reps - all managed separately. The process was not only inefficient but also impersonal. The challenge was to streamline communication across all touchpoints while delivering **hyper-relevant, personalized experiences**.

Different customer profiles - like restaurant owners, kiosk managers, and self-employed professionals - required different types of offers and communication styles. Manual coordination simply couldn't keep up.

#### **The AI-Driven Solution**

Over a 6 month rollout, we deployed a modular AI stack that modernized the entire customer engagement flow:

1.  **Onboarding**  
    QR codes, printed flyers, and app links guided users into WhatsApp flows. AI agents captured consent, preferences, and buying intent - all via natural conversation.  
      
    
2.  **Hyper-Personalized Targeting**  
    Customer profiles were enriched with purchase behavior, enabling **automated, customized product offers**. Messages were phrased in the user’s tone and timed for peak engagement.  
      
    
3.  **Smart Support**  
    WhatsApp became a full-service channel - handling inbound queries, routing support requests, and even managing callback requests, all through AI chatbots.  
      
    
4.  **Salesforce Assistant**  
    Sales reps received tools to quickly onboard customers via WhatsApp and even demonstrate how to use the channel during visits. This bridged the digital divide for many B2B clients.  
      
    
5.  **Last-Minute Promo Engine**  
    Perhaps the most innovative module: we built a **last-minute sales flow for near-expiry or perishable stock**. Store managers could simply send a photo or video of a product via WhatsApp - and our AI system would:  
      
    
    -   Extract product name, expiry date, and quantity  
          
        
    -   Automatically craft a time-sensitive deal  
          
        
    -   Send it instantly to relevant customer segments (like nearby restaurants)
        

This created a real-time "Flash Sale" capability, reducing waste and driving same-day pickups - especially valuable in the food and beverage sector.
